about the competetion

This event aims to get individuals to complete challenges and utilising their problem solving skills and knowledge to come up with strategic plans for completing their task.

timeline

The Competition will proceed in following 

  • 11th July

    ROUND 1: Let's Get Quizzical

    This round will contain total 45 multiple choice questions divided in 3 parts :
    ◾Part I - GK based questions
    ◾Part II - Based on Trending and Relevant Entertainment Content
    ◾Part III - Emojis challenge

  • 11th July

    ROUND 2: Scribbles

    The funniest game to play with friends online. This will be played on online platform skribbl.io in a group of 10-12 players.


  • ELIMINATION

  • 14th July

    ROUND 3: Final Round

    This round will have 2 segments :
    ◾Guess and Fetch
    ◾Face the Chaos (yesss, it's messy)

  • Winners
    Announced
